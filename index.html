Company Profile

IndTech Decision Labs SA

IndTech Decision Labs SA is a specialist independent consultancy that helps South African mines and heavy industrial operations make confident, de-risked technology decisions in the era of the Just Energy Transition (JET).

Accountability and reliability is important to us, do what you said you will do. If there is failure lets monitor it and implement a plan on how it will be resolved in future. While your OEM or suppliers look into performance assurance of their systems, we look into value assurance. Our non affiliations with any OEM guarantees to bias outcome in our review.

Performance Assurance vs Value Assurance

Aspect Performance Assurance (OEM / Supplier) Value Assurance (IndTech Decision Labs SA)
Primary FocusEquipment specifications, uptime, MTBF/MTTRReal business value: production impact, cost, safety, long-term stability
BiasVendor-alignedCompletely independent — no vendor affiliations
Failure HandlingReports failure and often provides temporary fixMonitors failure, quantifies impact (tonnes/hour lost), flags reoccurrence (>3×), records every attempted fix, and drives permanent resolution
TraceabilityLimited to warranty or contract scopeFull long-term traceability of failures, fixes, and outcomes
AccountabilityLimited to equipment performanceFull accountability — we monitor, report, and implement resolution plans

Why Independent Review Matters More Than Internal or OEM-Based Reviews

Internal reviews are often influenced by existing relationships, internal politics, or budget constraints. OEM-based reviews are naturally biased toward protecting the supplier’s position and may understate recurring issues that affect your production and profitability.

An independent review delivers truly unbiased, fact-based recommendations, metrics that reflect your actual operational and financial reality, full accountability, and defensible documentation for boards, funders, and regulators.
