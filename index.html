Value Assurance™

Linking Technology Performance to Measurable KPI and ROI Outcomes

Most technology providers validate system performance. We validate business impact.

Value Assurance™ is IndTech Decision Labs SA’s core methodology for ensuring that every technology investment delivers against defined operational KPIs, ROI targets, and long-term value creation objectives.

Executive Definition

Value Assurance™ = The structured measurement, validation, and enforcement of technology-driven outcomes against operational KPIs, financial ROI, and reliability targets.

Why It Matters

In capital-intensive mining environments, technical compliance does not guarantee financial return. Operations require certainty that investments:

  • Improve core production KPIs (throughput, tonnes/hour, equipment availability)
  • Deliver positive ROI within defined payback periods
  • Reduce OPEX through downtime elimination and maintenance efficiency
  • Protect CAPEX through validated, evidence-based decision-making

Performance Assurance vs Value Assurance™

How We Operationalise Value Assurance™

  1. KPI-Aligned Decision Review
    Validate proposed technologies against defined production and financial KPIs. Stress-test vendor claims against real operating conditions. Deliver Go / No-Go decisions based on ROI viability.
  2. ROI-Driven Technology Roadmap
    Align technology investments to production growth targets, cost optimisation strategies, and JET / ESG compliance requirements. Sequence initiatives based on ROI priority and risk exposure.
  3. FailureGuard™ (KPI & Reliability Engine)
    Tracks failures and maps them to downtime (hours lost), production loss (tonnes/hour), and cost impact (R/hour). Flags repeat failures (>3 occurrences) impacting KPI stability. Ensures corrective actions translate into sustained KPI improvement.
  4. Closed-Loop KPI & ROI Tracking
    Continuous monitoring of KPI performance vs baseline and ROI realised vs projected. Intervention until KPI targets are met and ROI is demonstrably achieved and sustained.

Measured Outcomes

Value Assurance™ delivers:

  • • Improved KPI performance (availability ↑, downtime ↓, throughput ↑)
  • • Accelerated ROI realisation (shorter payback periods, higher IRR)
  • • Reduced OPEX leakage (elimination of recurring failures and inefficiencies)
  • • CAPEX risk mitigation (validated, defensible investment decisions)

Independent by Design

All KPI tracking, ROI validation, and performance reporting are conducted independently of OEMs and vendors, ensuring no bias in performance measurement, transparent ROI validation, and decisions aligned strictly to shareholder and operational value.
